Assistive Hand Device

Designed a low-cost assistive rehabilitation device for stroke patients experiencing early to mid-stage hand spasticity.

Focused on improving comfort, accessibility, and one-handed usability through a compliant open-frame finger extension mechanism.

Worked on rapid prototyping, iterative testing, and user-focused mechanical design for rehabilitation support.
